# Kin Design System: Typography & Colors

Here is a breakdown of the typography and colors used across the project, including where and why each specific color is applied.

## 1. Typography

The project uses three main Google Fonts to achieve its premium, editorial aesthetic:

- **Inter**: The primary sans-serif font used for most body text, headings, and functional UI elements. It provides clean readability.
- **Space Grotesk**: Used for specific stylized labels, buttons, and uppercase tracking elements to add a modern, structural feel.
- **Syne**: Used for large, bold, statement headings (extra bold weights) to create high visual impact.

**Common Typographic Patterns:**
- **Eyebrow Labels / Accents**: Very small text (`10px` or `11px`), heavily tracked (`tracking-[0.4em]` or `0.5em`), uppercase, and bold.
- **Section Titles**: Large, elegant serif or sans-serif combinations, sometimes incorporating italicized connecting words (e.g., *in* Nature).

## 2. Color Palette & Usage

### Dark Backgrounds (Primary Theme)
- **`#080808`**: The main global body background. It gives the site its core dark, premium look.
- **`#050505`**: Slightly darker than the main bg. Used to create subtle depth in specific sections like the **Loader**, **Visual Grid Section**, and **Ways To Mix** section.
- **`#0c0c0c`**: Used as an inner container background in the **Hero Section** to create a card-like floating effect against the main `#080808` background.
- **`#020202`**: Almost pure black, used in the **Final CTA** section to maximize contrast at the bottom of the page.

### Light Backgrounds (Contrast Sections)
- **`#f6f6f6`**: The main background for light-themed sections like the **Product Section**.
- **`#f8f8f8`**: Used in the **CTA Section** to provide a clean, airy contrast to the dark theme.
- **`#e5e5e5`**: Used for product card backgrounds and borders in the light sections to create soft separation from the `#f6f6f6` background.

### Accent Colors (Highlights & Interactions)
- **`#ccff00`** (Neon Yellow) & **`#bef32e`** (Lime Green): These are the primary brand accent colors. They are used interchangeably for:
  - Text selection background.
  - Small decorative separator lines (`1px` height).
  - Hover states on buttons (e.g., changing from white to neon green on hover).
  - Highlighting specific key words or labels (like "PURE BOTANICALS" or "The New Standard").

### Text & Typography Colors
- **`#ffffff` (White)**: Primary text color for dark sections.
- **`#000000` (Black)**: Primary text color for light sections, and used for text inside white/neon buttons or text selections.
- **`#888888` / `#666666`**: Muted dark grays used for paragraph text and descriptions in the light sections (like the Product Section).
- **`#cccccc` / `#bbbbbb`**: Muted light grays used for secondary text, numeric indicators (e.g., "01 / Pure"), and subtitles in dark sections.

### UI Elements (Scrollbars & Borders)
- **`#333333`**: Custom scrollbar thumb color, turning to `#555555` on hover for a subtle interaction cue.
- **`white/5` (`rgba(255,255,255,0.05)`)**: Used for very faint, elegant divider lines between dark sections (like the top border of the Ways To Mix section).
